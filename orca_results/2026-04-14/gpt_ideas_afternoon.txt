Catalyst timing: 2026-04-10 | Status: Recently Triggered

Entry trigger: Reclaim and hold above the April 10 open at $3.99 / the April 9 close at $4.01, which would begin filling the post-catalyst reversal gap.
Invalidation condition: Loss of the April 10 low at $3.63, or new evidence that Replimune's setback does not improve Amtagvi's near-term competitive position.
Target / repricing path: Partial catch-up back toward the April 10 intraday high at $4.31, with first objective a return to the pre-rejection area around $4.00-$4.01.
Horizon / time stop: 1 to 3 weeks, before the estimated early-to-mid May earnings window.
Cleanest expression: Iovance is the cleanest listed expression because Reuters tied Replimune's melanoma CRL specifically to reduced near-term competition for Amtagvi, while larger oncology names such as Bristol Myers are far more diversified and dilute the read-through.
What already moved: Replimune fell 58% in extended trading after the FDA rejection.
Evidence of lag: Iovance fell 7.73% on April 10, closing at $3.70 after opening at $3.99, even though Reuters framed the same event as favorable for Amtagvi's competitive position.
Thesis: This is a fresh, direct competitor-removal catalyst in the exact indication where Iovance already has an FDA-approved product. Reuters explicitly linked the Replimune rejection to a better near-term competitive setup for Amtagvi, while Iovance's own February update said Amtagvi demand drove roughly 30% quarterly revenue growth and improving gross margin. The stock's immediate downside reversal after a nominally positive read-through creates the underreaction setup. A move back into the April 9-10 gap zone would be the simplest tactical catch-up path.
Counter-thesis: Iovance had already rallied 15.56% on April 9 before the FDA decision, so some of the read-through may already have been anticipated, and the company is still early in commercialization despite the strong fourth-quarter update.
R2 flags: Verify the exact earnings date, current Amtagvi launch trajectory through April, whether April 9 already priced much of the competitor-failure setup, and whether any follow-up commentary changes the competitive significance of Replimune's CRL.
Key sources: Reuters on Replimune's April 10 CRL and Iovance read-through; Iovance February 24, 2026 business update; Yahoo Finance historical prices for April 9-14, 2026.
